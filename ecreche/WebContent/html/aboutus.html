ABOUT US

BLOOMING BIRDS is a long established creche that is a highly reputable provider of quality CHILDCARE. The creche aims to provide a happy, caring, secure educationally based Environment for each and every child . The ethos at Blooming Birds is one of "Children First" and the belief that every child is unique child, one who is constantly learning and evolving into a resilient, capable, confident individual. The ethos is achieved through the development of a Positive Partnership with parents and by providing an environment which is sensitiveand responsive to the child's feelings, need and interests.

We provide a number of programmes that ensure an all-round development of the child. The success of each programme depends greatly on flexibilty, understanding and a balanced approach to learning through fun, games and structures schedule.
