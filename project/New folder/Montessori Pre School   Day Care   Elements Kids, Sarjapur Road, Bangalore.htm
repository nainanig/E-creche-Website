Elements Kids Montessori Preschool, Day Care (Creche) & After School Activities

 

 

Elements Kids is a professionally managed institute which strives to impart high standards of education and care to children. The services at Elements Kids includes a Preschool which follows the Montessori pedagogy (parallel and alternative to Kindergarten, Playschool); Day Care (Creche) and comprehensive after school activities  (Fun Forest Club) for children; all in the age group of 6 months to 15 years.

 

 

At Elements Kids, we believe that your child should be safe & happy  in an environment that is fun, educational & nurturing while receiving quality care suited to his/her development needs. The naturally lit large spaces in the institute along with the scenic outdoor areas spread over 12000 sqft of land (on Sarjapur Road, Bangalore), help your child connect with nature and provides a conducive atmosphere for learning and growth.
