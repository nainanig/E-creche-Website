YOU ASK…

aiSia is a nursery where you can learn Chinese. Is it open to anybody ?

Yes, our concept of nursery is open to all parents who want their child discovers the mix. Chinese is an optional language among other languages spoken in our structure. Parents can choose this option in consultation with our educators.

Why enrolling my child in aiSia crèche rather than another one ?

aiSia International Crèche offers an original concept of international nursery with additional assets

  • - An additional language and Culture
  • - Additional Comfort and Safety
  • - Additional Flexibility of the opening hours

Enrolling my child in aiSia, yes, but after ?

If you chose the Chinese for your child, aiSia International Crèche proposes to continue learning this language by attending the courses offered by aiSia Lingua's teachers.
