Children Melissa Crèche

The safety and wellbeing of your children is paramount, so you’ll find our wonderfully equipped crèche is operated by UK childcare specialists Worldwide Kids Company  who work to Ofsted standards, policies and procedures.

Our UK qualified nursery staff look after children as young as four months and up to four years old. There are two main locations within the Resort, one close to Sani Beach Hotel and a short walking distance from Porto Sani Village and Sani Asterias Suites and one at Sani Beach Club.

The Melissa Crèche is very well-equipped with toys and there is a good programme of indoor and outdoor activities for all age groups - babies, toddlers & pre-schoolers. There is a staff to children ratio of 1:3 for children from 4 months to 23 months and 1:4 for children between the ages of 2 and 3 years.

  • Melissa Crèche operated by UK childcare specialists Worldwide Kids Company
  • The Melissa Crèche is well equipped with toys for indoor & outdoor activities 
  • Managed by qualified nursery staff according to the UK standards & regulations
  • Staff to children ratio of 1:3 for children from 4 months to 23 months and 1:4 for children between the ages of 2 and 4 years
  • Special programmed activities each week specifically designed for babies, toddlers & pre-schoolers 
  • Our first priority is the safety of the children and our childcare staff are selected and trained with this in mind 
  • Most baby supplies such as nappies, formula milk and baby food can be purchased locally. If you require a particular brand it is advisable to bring your own 
  • Toys and equipment comply with European safety standards
  • Written policies exist on Health and Hygiene, medication, emergency procedures, special needs, equal opportunities, child protection etc 
  • Bottle warmers and baby sterilizers are provided upon request

Pre-bookings are advised and available here.

Cancellation policy:

Please note that we do operate childcare facilities under strict carer: child ratios. As such spaces are limited. For any crèche bookings we do expect at least 24 hours notice of changes otherwise you will be charged for the session.

Prices

4mths – 23 months: 35€ per session or 50€ for 2x3 hour sessions in one day. €25 for the 13:00-15:00 Lunch session.

24mths – 4 years: 28€ per session or 40€ for 2x3 hour sessions in one day. €25 for the 13:00-15:00 Lunch session.

For children aged 4 + is complimentary from 10:00-18:00

Lunch and Dinner sessions

Lunch and Dinner for children will be supervised at 13:00-15:00 and 18:30-21:30 in the main buffet restaurant and be charged to your room where appropriate.

Lunch sessions  are complimentary for children over 4 years old as long as they stay on  full board basis. The meal  allowance of 12.50 Euros is deducted from total cost for all children staying on Halfboard basis. Creche lunch costs 25 Euros.All charges  are payable at the end of your holiday.

For more information please visit Worldwide Kids Company

Baby swimming lessons (June-September)

Join us now and learn the way to teach your little ones the joys of water with safety! 

The lessons will be carried out at the indoor swimming pool of MySpa by our certified and experienced partners “Pirates of the Swimming Pool” and they are available for toddlers between 6 months and 4 years old. 

Each programme will have five sessions in total from Monday to Friday , one per day,  and the duration of each session will be 30 minutes.

For more information please visit “MySpa” reception at Sani Beach Hotel from 09:00a.m. to 12:00a.m.

Babysitting Facilities

> Additional fee applicable
> Reservations required
> Maximum 3 children

Evening babysitting can be requested in advance. For prior requests this will be confirmed on arrival in resort. This is subject to staff availability. Babysitting costs €10 per hour and is payable directly to babysitters. If the babysitter is required after 10.50pm there is a surcharge of 15 Euro’s to pay for a taxi for the babysitter.
