Booking

To place a booking or for more details please email or call our Mobile Creche Team on:

0207 127 8112 or e-mail info@tigerlilycreche.co.uk

We'll be delighted to answer all your questions and provide a wide range of FREE advice without obligation to book.

What costs are involved?

Tigerlily offer a range of affordable and professional crèche packages designed to suit all kinds of budgets and requirements. Our prices are based on both the number and ages of children present and theme or entertainment required. Simply give us a call on 0207 127 8112 or email info@tigerlilycreche.co.uk and we'll assess your requirements and provide you with a competitive quote.

What if only a small number of children require care?

For smaller events we can provide Event Nannies – who can provide care for up to 3 children in a range of different environments. Full crèche equipment is not provided with this service, which keeps costs to a minimum and allows the client to provide activities / toys of their own.

What happens once I've booked?

  • We'll take a small deposit
  • Provide you with a dedicated Tigerlily Planner to help you every step of the way
  • Research your venue and conduct a full risk assessment / opportunity analysis
  • Work with you in selecting the best theme, package and equipment for your crèche
  • Personally select the ideal crèche team for the day
  • Provide comprehensive insurance

On the day itself

  • We will transform the space you have chosen into the themed play area discussed and planned with you. This will be fully equipped with soft play areas, games, toys, books, dressing up clothes etc
  • Our staff will meet and greet all parents and brief them fully on the day ahead and the pick-up and visiting protocols and security checks
  • All parents will receive a clear itinerary to help them decide when to visit or pick up
  • Each child will receive a personalised name badge and gift pack
  • Catering and refreshments are provided throughout the event
  • All crèche equipment will be removed after the event and the space left clean and tidy
  • The crèche manager will be on-hand throughout the day
